Lost in Boston

Proposal Book

Spring 2013

This was a book designed to propose a timeline installation in Boston's Downtown Crossing. The timeline allows the viewer to experience, and value, this lost something, the old marketplace of Downtown Crossing, as well as educate them on the history.
