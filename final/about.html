My name is Juliana Farnham and I'm currently a junior in Graphic Design at Massachusetts College of Art and Design in Boston. My interests lie mainly in print work, in particular book design. I recently came back from a study abroad trip to the Netherlands and was absolutely inspired by Dutch print design. Hopefully that influence will begin to show in my upcoming print and book work. I am from West Orange, NJ but currently live in Boston, MA. Below is a link to my resume if you would like further information about me and my experience.
