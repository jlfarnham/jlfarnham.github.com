How Species Interact

Book

Fall 2012

This was a book designed to inform and educate a reader about species interaction in nature. Using simple, but fun illustrations that appeal to both an older and younger demographic, and arrow infographics to describe the types of interactions species have, I created this book to give detailed information without being “textbook.”
