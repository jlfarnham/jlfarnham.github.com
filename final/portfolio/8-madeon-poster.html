Madeon's Pop Culture

Poster

Fall 2011

This was a poster designed to illustrate the song ‘Pop Culture’ by Madeon. It is a song that samples 37 other songs. Each album for each song is represented on this poster in bright and vivid style which mimics the fun, pop feeling the song evokes.
