Juliana Farnham Designs

Doritos Ads

Magazine Ads

Fall 2012

This was a series of magazine ads for the always wild brand of Doritos chips. The concept of this ad series was the orange fingers Doritos eaters get when eating their product, and hands as a meal themselves when all the chips are gone. It takes a slightly dark humor approach to traditional food advertisements, which is right within Doritos style, brand, and demographic for a younger, wilder audience.
