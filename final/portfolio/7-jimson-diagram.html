Jimson Weed Lifecycle

Diagram

Spring 2011

This was a simple illustration to explain visually the lifecycle of a jimson weed plant, from beginning to end, and then back again. Each stage of the plant's life is represented on the same stalk, with numbers corresponding to the steps in order.
