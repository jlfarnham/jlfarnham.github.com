Train Timetable

Diagram

Spring 2012

This was a redesign of an existing train timetable by Northeast Regional train service. Where before it was clunky, full of extraneous and repeating information, and no glancibility whatsoever, the timetable now not only looks aesthetically much more pleasing but is a much more functional and glancible piece.
