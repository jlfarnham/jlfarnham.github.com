Theme Park Accidents

Website

Spring 2013

This was a website prototype created to tell the story of theme park accidents of the last two decades. Using a huge chunk of data about these incidents, with the help of my partner Maria Lauer, we filtered and sorted the data and came to a few conclusions about how to be both safe and unsafe at a theme park. Our website acts like a park map, whereby clicking on a ride or attraction, you can learn more about theme park safety and specific incidents of deaths and injuries at theme parks.
