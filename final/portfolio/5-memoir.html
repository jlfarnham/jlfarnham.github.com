6 Word Memoir

Poster

Fall 2011

This was a print piece designed to embody my ‘6 word memoir’ about myself. For whatever reason at the time, I chose to make my ‘memoir’ about the cats that I've owned and came up with the phrase, “I had a cat, then another.” The colors of the two statements mirror the color of my cats.
