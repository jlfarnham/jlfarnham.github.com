Road Safety Stamps

Stamps

Spring 2012

This was a series of four stamps designed to raise awareness about road safety, in this case the “don'ts” of driving. Using the same visual system and imagery with small twists corresponding to each “don't,” I was able to create a cohesive, interesting stamp design that also informs the audience.
