Virtues of Design

Book

Spring 2012

This was a book designed to compare two pieces of text side-by-side: Gui Bonsieppe's “Virtues of Design” and Paul Rand's “Integrity and Invention”. Both focus on the ‘virtues’ of design, or what makes design good to each specific author.
