Juliana Farnham Designs

'Let's Go' Onomatopoeia

Poster, Score, Animation

Spring 2012

This was a multi-component project in which the song ‘Let's Go’ by Mychael Danna and Devotchka was interpreted using the onomatopoeia from the sounds of the instruments. This onomatopoeia was then used to create a musical onomatopoeia ‘score’ for the song, a poster which represents the lively, circus-like feeling of the song, and an animation to the song itself.
