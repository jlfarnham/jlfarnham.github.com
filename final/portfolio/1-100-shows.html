100 Television Shows

Diagram, Animation, and iPhone App

Fall 2012

This was a multi-component project designed to take 100 everyday things and transform that data into a poster, an animation, and an iPhone app. I chose 108 TV shows I've watched in my life with attributes of name, airdate, genre, subgenre, type of viewer, who I watch it with, and when I watched it. Those seven attributes are the basis of all three components.
