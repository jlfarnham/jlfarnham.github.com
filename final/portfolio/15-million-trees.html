Juliana Farnham Designs

Million Trees NYC PSA

Advertisement, Packaging

Fall 2012

This was a series of ads designed for the organization Million Trees NYC to feature as posters, in subways, bus shelters, etc. This organization's mission is to plant 1 million trees in NYC, increasing the city's urban forest and providing benefits to the city. My goal in this series was to shed light on both the more commonplace benefits of trees in urban environments, but also the less well-known benefits trees provide to people.
