How to Shuffle with Bridge

Instructional Diagram

Fall 2012

This was an instructional diagram created to inform the viewer on how to do an action or task: in this case, how to shuffle cards and end in a bridge. The challenge was to show a physical act through a static 2-D medium and without words to give directions or steps.
